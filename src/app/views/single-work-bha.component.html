Website re-design & build

A major re-design and build project for the official governing body of horse racing in the UK.

The BHA website is extensive; it contains information for industry insiders, punters and first time race-goers alike. The site is authoritive, progressive and simple to use, reflecting the aims of the regulatory authority. As a portal for information ranging from disqualified jockeys to racecourse locations, the site needed to be easy to use in all situations.

Simple and super effective navigation making a vast collection of information easily accessible.

No. 1 source of information ranging from disqualified jockeys to racecourse locations, all at a punters finger tips.

Leveraging techology to encourge more people to engage with the sport of horseracing.
