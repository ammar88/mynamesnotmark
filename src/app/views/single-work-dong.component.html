Website design & build

A nationwide offshore wind campaign and online resource created for Dong Energy UK.

Over the last ten years, offshore wind in the UK has soared. Dong Energy UK comissioned Firedog to build a print and digital campaign to help the UK's general public find out about the continuing development of this renewable energy. Designed to educated people on how it’s managed to cut costs and boost efficiency, growing into an important part of Britain’s energy mix.

Focused and well segmented content via clean and easy to navigate UI.
